Short Bio

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade do Porto, under the supervision of Prof. Mário Florido. Having started in September of 2018, and planning to finishing on July 2023, my PhD topic is about program verification using gradual typing and intersection types.

I started my joint bachelor and master in Network and Information Systems Engineering in 2012 and finished in 2017, also from Faculdade de Ciência da Universidade do Porto.

Research Interests

My interests lie in the study of lambda calculus and types.
